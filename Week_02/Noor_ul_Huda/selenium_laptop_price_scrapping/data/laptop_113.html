EPEAT certifies electronic products based on sustainability impacts across their life cycle including requirements for energy efficiency and end-of-life management. An EPEAT registered product can also meet optional more ambitious criteria including reduction of fluorinated greenhouse gases (F-GHGs). Products meeting 50% - 74% optional criteria are 'Silver' and those meeting 75% or more are 'Gold'.
